The University of Arizona Thesis Template

This is a LaTeX template for students writing a thesis at The University of Arizona. This version was last updated in 2013 to match the current Graduate College requirements. In addtion, a new class format for writting a dissertation proposal was added.

Please see README.txt for further information.
